DVSN Fan Site!

DVSN (stylized as dvsn and pronounced "division") is a Canadian R&B duo, composed of singer Daniel Daley and producer Nineteen85. The duo specializes in narcotic slow jams featuring sexually frank lyrics, often in falsetto. DVSN is signed to OVO Sound.
